Course website: Melusine

Melusine cooking class part 1

This was the introductory course. We learned basic techniques for cutting vegetables, keeping the knife sharp with a steel or whetstone, balancing the basic flavors (salty, sweet, sour, umami) and adding aromatic flavors with spices and herbs. We used locally grown vegetables, learned how to use beans and tofu, made main courses, desserts and baked bread. We also had a nutrition review where we focused on the vitamins and minerals you should consider getting as a vegan.

Melusine cooking class part 2

We delved deeper and learned some more advanced techniques. We learned about fermentation and made our own kimchi. We learned more advanced cooking techniques, made our own broth, etc. We learned about plating and garnishing.

Melusine cooking class part 3

Even more advanced techniques. We learned more about how to combine different flavors and textures. We practiced more advanced layering techniques. Last week we prepared to serve food to 9 invited guests, which was very successful and appreciated.
